Why Cook with us?

Free Recipes

All our recipes are available here free of charge. Cooking should be something that everyone can do and enjoy. At Love 2 Cook, we believe everyone has the ability to cook and recipes should be open source for all.

Simple to Follow

Love 2 Cook recipes are structured in a way that make them straightforward and simple to understand. Each recipe comes with step-by-step guides that are easy to follow, taking out all the guesswork of cooking.

Low Cost Recipes

As well as being free, all our recipes aim to cost effective to suit all budgets. Each of our recipes outline the average cost of each ingredient, meaning you can leave the instant noodles on the shelf.

Health is Wealth

For cooking to be cost effective, it doesn't mean you have to sacrifice on quality. Healthy food can be tasty and just as inexpensive as your 2 for 1 ready meals. With a little time and effort, you can improve your health and wellbeing with these super easy and healthy recipes.
