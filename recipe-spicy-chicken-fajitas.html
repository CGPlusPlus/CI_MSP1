Spicy Chicken Fajitas

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 10 min

Total Time: 25 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp / 30ml olive oil
  • 2 chicken breasts, cut into strips
  • 1 large onion, cut into strips
  • 1 red pepper, cut into strips
  • 1 green pepper, cut into strips
  • 1 garlic clove, finely chopped / minced
  • 1 heaped tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp chilli powder
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 6 flour tortillas
  • guacamole (optional)
  • cheddar cheese, grated
  • fresh salsa

Method

  1. Heat up the oven to 180C for heating your tortillas
  2. Put a large wok on a high heat and add your olive oil.
  3. Add the sliced chicken, season with salt and pepper and fry for 6 mins until cooked on the outside.
  4. Next add the onions, peppers and garlic cloves and fry for 2-3 mins until veg softens slightly.
  5. Add the seasoning mix and cook for further 7-8 minutes until fully cooked
  6. Wrap tortillas in tinfoil and add them to oven for 2 minutes to warm throughly
  7. Add the lime juice to fajita mix near the end for a zesty kick to your fajitas.
  8. Check chicken is throughly cooked and serve with salsa and a salad (optional)
  9. Enjoy!
