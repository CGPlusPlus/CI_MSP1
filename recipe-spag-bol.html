Spaghetti Bolognese

Prep Time: 20 mins

Cook Time: 1 hr 20min

Total Time: 1hr 40min

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp / 15ml olive oil
  • 4 smoked streaky bacon rashers, finely chopped
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 large carrots, finely chopped
  • 3 stalks of celery, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped / minced
  • 3 sprigs of rosemary / 2 tsp dried rosemary
  • 500g mined beef
  • 2 tins plum tomatoes
  • 1 beef stock cube
  • 1/2 pack of fresh basil, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 frsh/dried bay leaves
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 125ml red wine (optional, but recommended)
  • parmesan to serve
  • 500g spaghetti
  • crusy bread / garlic bread (optional)

Method

  1. Put a large saucepan on a medium heat and add your olive oil.
  2. Add the finely chopped rashers and fry for 10 mins until golden and crisp.
  3. Reduce the heat slightly and add the onions, celery and carrots, garlic cloves and the leaves from 2-3 sprigs rosemary, then fry for 10 mins until veg softens.
  4. Increase the heat to medium-high, add minced beef and cook stirring for about 5 mins until the meat is browned all over.
  5. Add the tins of plum tomatoes, the finely chopped basil leaves, dried oregano, bay leaves, tomato pur√©e, beef stock cube and 125ml red wine. Stir with a wooden spoon, breaking up the plum tomatoes.
  6. Bring this to the boil, reduce to a simmer and cover with a lid. Cook for 50 mins stirring occasionally, until sauce becomes rich and thick.
  7. When the bolognese is nearly done, cook your spaghetti following the pack instructions.
  8. Drain the spaghetti and either stir into the bolognese sauce, or serve the sauce on top. Serve with grated parmesan on top, the remaining basil leaves and garlic bread, if you like.
  9. Enjoy!
